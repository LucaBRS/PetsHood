People

Petshood is an association that deals with the administration and management of three kennels on the territory of Emilia Romagna. Born from the love of the founders for animals, its area of intervention is to combine the pursuit of animal welfare and responsible management of their spaces. We want to spread a culture of proper animal care and we propose a model of kennels as a place open to the public, a place of comparison with other animals.
